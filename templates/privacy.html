Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the Service, we collect the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the features of Spendly — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and letting you filter and manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Service for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access or disclosure. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all your data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (hosting, storage) to operate the Service, but these providers are contractually prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on our site. We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries promptly.
